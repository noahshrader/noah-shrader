Make signals from noise.

I'm a product designer living in Orlando, Florida. I'm currently Head of Design at StreamYard, a product that makes it easy for anyone to become a successful live streamer, all from the browser. Before that, I worked at Automattic shaping experiences for WordPress, a platform that powers more than 35% of the web.

Over the last 12 years, I have worked on both small and large cross-functional teams as a senior and staff product designer, team lead, and on a few occasions, product manager. I have a passion for unlocking and unblocking, and bringing clarity to complex problems.

Good design is intentional, focuses on the end user, and ultimately motivates people to take action. Good product design is a combination of devotion and empathy for customer needs (researching and connecting with the problems to be solved), good visual strategy (translating ideas well to actually solve those problems), and leadership (making signals from noisy inputs, building alignment and driving meaningful progress forward).
